Our Story

Built on Experience.
Driven by Excellence.

Nova Pools was founded by a certified pool technician who spent years maintaining the pools of Jamaica's finest hotels, resorts, and luxury villas. What began as a passion for perfection became a mission: to bring that same gold-standard care to residential and commercial properties across the island.

Every pool we service is treated as if it belongs to a five-star property. We are not simply cleaning water — we are protecting your investment, safeguarding your family's health, and delivering an experience that reflects the standard your property deserves.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Pool Care,
Delivered Professionally

From routine maintenance to specialist treatments — every service performed with hotel-grade precision and full documentation.

Most Popular

Weekly & Bi-weekly
Maintenance Plans

Our flagship service — a complete, recurring pool care package that keeps your pool pristine week after week. Includes skimming, vacuuming, chemical balancing, filter checks, and a detailed service report after every visit.

Book This Service
01
Skimming & Debris Removal

Complete surface and basket cleaning each visit — leaves, insects, and organic debris removed before they sink, stain, or compromise water chemistry.

02
Brushing & Vacuuming

Thorough wall, floor, step, and cove brushing with professional vacuuming to eliminate algae footholds and sedimentation buildup.

03
Water Testing & Chemical Balancing

Multi-point water chemistry analysis and precise chemical treatment — pH, chlorine, alkalinity, and calcium hardness maintained at optimal levels.

04
Filter Cleaning & Maintenance

Regular backwashing, cartridge cleaning, and deep filter service to keep your filtration system running at peak efficiency.

05
Tile & Waterline Cleaning

Professional removal of calcium deposits, body oils, sunscreen films, and mineral staining for a flawless, resort-quality waterline.

06
Equipment Inspection

Full inspection of pumps, motors, heaters, chlorinators, and automation systems — catching issues early prevents costly emergency repairs.

07
Algae Treatment & Shock

Targeted super-chlorination and algaecide treatments to eliminate green, black, and mustard algae — with a prevention program to stop recurrence.

08
Pool Opening & Closing

Comprehensive seasonal startup and shutdown services — full system inspection, chemical startup, equipment checks, and complete documentation.

09
Advanced Water Lab Testing

In-depth water quality analysis beyond standard testing — full mineral panel, phosphate levels, and a complete water profile report.
